Privacy Policy for Datarai.com

Last Updated: 23 Feb 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Datarai.com. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. We collect information in the following ways:

  • Email Address: We collect your email address when you sign up for an account.
  • We track how you use features on our platform to improve our services. This data is stored in our database.
  • We do not store your uploaded files in our database. Instead, they are stored in your local storage.
  • Data used for analysis is temporarily sent to third-party services (e.g., Gemini API) for processing.
  • User information, feature usage data, and messages exchanged with the AI are stored in Firebase.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect from all our services for the following purposes:

  • To identify users and personalize their experience.
  • To track feature usage and improve our platform.
  • To process user-provided data for analysis and visualization using AI models like Gemini.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your data with:

  • AI processing services (e.g., Gemini API) to generate responses based on your queries.
  • Service providers such as Firebase for authentication, storage, and analytics.

Data Storage and Security

  • User data is stored securely in Firebase and local storage on your device.
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Your Rights

Since user data is stored in local storage and Firebase, we are still working on providing tools for users to:

  • Delete their data upon request.
  • Export their data if needed.

These features are not currently available but will be implemented in the future.

Children's Privacy

Datarai.com is available to users of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, we will notify users via email or through our website.
